ABOUT US

Welcome to CoronaHealthCare, on this website we are going to revile all the news about Wuhan Virus knownst as coronavirus or COVID-19. We will also inform about Why it happened, how to shield ourselves from it, and we will be providing help to people who live in Malaysia in case of emergency. Together we strive to combat the virus, ensure the safety of individuals by following the advice we provide to remain protected.

OUR VALUES

Our values are about trying to help people to keep the highest possible level of health during these hard times, and our mission to promote a way to boost our immunity to be able to fight the virus, keep the individual safe as we serve enough knowledge, and guides to follow. We are individually and collectively committed to putting these values into practice. As we hope, our website will fulfill its mission towards the IIUM community. STAY SAFE EVERYONE!
